# Fundraising Operations Workflow

## Core Workflow Stages

1. Campaign Planning
2. Prospect Segmentation
3. Outreach Coordination
4. Donation Capture
5. Acknowledgement
6. Stewardship
7. Financial Reconciliation
8. Executive Reporting

## Campaign Planning

### Inputs
- annual fundraising goals
- board priorities
- sponsorship targets
- prior year performance
- campaign calendar

### Key Activities
- define campaign objective
- establish revenue target
- assign ownership
- define target donor segments
- determine ask strategy
- align reporting cadence

### Outputs
- campaign brief
- prospect list
- sponsorship inventory
- revenue forecast

## Control Points

- no campaign launches without assigned owner
- sponsorship inventory must be approved before outreach
- reporting definitions must be agreed before launch

## Common Risks

- duplicate donor outreach
- unclear ownership
- inconsistent donor records
- untracked pledges
- weak stewardship follow-up

## Recommended Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Development Lead | campaign strategy and execution |
| Board Chair | leadership alignment |
| Treasurer | financial reconciliation |
| CRM Admin | data integrity |
| Sponsorship Lead | partner outreach and fulfillment |
| Executive Sponsor | escalation support |
